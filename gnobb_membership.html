GNOBB Membership

About Membership

GNOBB welcomes all researchers and professionals in the field of Biological Sciences/Agricultural Sciences to be a part of this organization. A Master's degree in a relevant subject is the minimum requirement to be a member. GNOBB offers three kinds of membership choices to interested scientists/professionals.

Life Membership

Paid membership-one-time payment of 100$/10,000 BDT

Get Started
Popular

Premium Membership

Paid membership: $50 / 5,000 BDT every ten years. The Premium Membership is valid for 10 years.

Advantages of Life/Premium Membership

  1. Life/Premium members will be eligible to make their profile public in the GNOBB database. General members can have a profile in the GNOBB database but cannot make it public.
  2. Life/Premium members will enjoy reduced registration fees for any events organized by GNOBB.
  3. Life/Premium members will have privilege over general members for participating in limited-capacity workshops and seminars organized by GNOBB.
  4. A Short/Phy./Premium member will be provided with a GNOBB membership card.
  5. Life/Premium members will get privileges over general members for travel grants, research grants, training, internships, etc. provided/organized by GNOBB.

For questions about membership, please email: contact@gnobb.org
